STATEMENT  OF  LEGAL  EFFECT

Clause  2  :  This  clause  amends  section  19  of  the  Medical  Ordinance  (Chapter  105)
(hereinafter  referred  to  as  the  “principal  enactment”)  in  order  to  add  certain  matters  in
respect  of  which  regulations  can  be  made  by  the  Minister.

Clause  3  :  This  clause  amends  section  19C  of  the  principal  enactment  and  the  legal
effect  of  the  section  as  amended  is  to  provide  for  the  appointment  of  a  Committee  to
advice  the  Minister  regarding  any  recommendation  made  by  the  Medical  Council  in
respect  of  any  recognized  university  or  institution  under  subsection  (1)  of  section  19C.

Clause  4  :  This  clause  amends  section  19E  of  the  principal  enactment  for  better

explanation  of  the  term  “any  university  or  institution”.

Clause 5 : This clause amends section 29 of the principal enactment and is consequential

to  the  amendment  made  by  clause  2.
