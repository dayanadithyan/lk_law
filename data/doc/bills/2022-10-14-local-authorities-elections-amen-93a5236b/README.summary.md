- The document is titled “The Gazette of the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka” for October 14, 2022.
- It includes a Bill to amend the Local Authorities Elections Ordinance (Chapter 262), presented by Hon. Premnath C. Dolawatte, M.P. for Colombo District.
- Key amendments include the removal and substitution of subsection (2) of section 28, requiring political parties and independent groups to submit two nomination papers with 25% youth and 50% women candidates.
- The Commissioner must publish minimum numbers for women and youth candidates in nomination papers.
- Subsections (2D) and (2E) of section 28, and subsection (2) of section 11 of the Principal Enactment, are repealed and amended.
- The principle of evenly distributing women candidates among adult and young candidates is stipulated.
- The term “Youth Candidates” applies to both male and female candidates aged 18-35.
- In case of text inconsistencies, the Sinhala text will prevail.
