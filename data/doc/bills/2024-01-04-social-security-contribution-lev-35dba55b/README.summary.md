### Key Amendments:

1. **Clause 2**: 
   - **Amendment To Section 4 of the Principal Enactment**:
     - Decreases the turnover threshold for Social Security Contribution Levy registration from 120 million rupees to 60 million rupees per annum.
     - Effective from January 1, 2024.

2. **Clause 3**: 
   - **Amendment To Section 5 of the Principal Enactment**:
     - Updates registration turnover thresholds to align with the amended Section 4.
     - Specifies conditions for turnover exceeding relevant thresholds before and after January 1, 2024.

3. **General Provision**:
   - **In Case of Inconsistency**: Sinhala text prevails over the Tamil text.
