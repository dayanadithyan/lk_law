STATEMENT  OF  LEGAL  EFFECT

Clause  2:  This  clause  amends  section  14  of  the  Immigrants  and  Emigrants  Act
(Chapter  351)  and  the  legal  effect  of  that  clause  is  to  increase  the  period  of  a  visa  and  to
make  provisions  for  the  Minister  to  determine  the  period  of  permanent  residence  visa.
