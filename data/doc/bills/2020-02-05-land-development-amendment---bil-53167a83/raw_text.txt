2. Rule  1(b)  and  rule  2  of  the  Third  Schedule  of  the
principal Act should be replaced with the following rules:-

“1(b). Title to a holding for the purposes of Section 72
shall  devolve  on  the  relatives  of  the  permit  holder  or
owner in the order of priority in which they are respectively
mentioned  in  the  subjoined  table,  the  older  being
preferred to the younger if there are more relatives than
one in any group:

Provided that nothing in this rule should give priority
to the older person when a land has been developed by
other person than the older person.
