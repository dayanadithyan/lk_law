STATEMENT  OF  LEGAL  EFFECT

Clause  2:  This  clause  amends  the  long  title  to  the  Sri  Lanka  Land  Reclamation  and
Development  Corporation  Act,  No.  15  of  1968  and  the  legal  effect  of  that  section  is  to
change  the  name  of  the    “Sri  Lanka  Land  Reclamation  and  Development  Corporation”  to
“Sri  Lanka  Land    Development  Corporation”.

Clauses  3  &  4:    The  amendments  made  by  these  Clauses  are  consequential  to  the

amendment  made  by  Clause  2.

(hereinafter referred to as the “principal enactment”) is hereby
amended by the substitution for the words “Sri Lanka Land
Reclamation and Development Corporation” wherever such
words appear in the Long title of the words “Sri Lanka Land
Development Corporation” .
