AN ACT TO AMEND THE LAND DEVELOPMENT ORDINANCE
(CHAPTER 464)

BE it  enacted by the Parliament of the Democratic Socialist
Republic of Sri Lanka as follows:—

1. This  Act  may  be  cited  as  the  Land  Development

Short  title

(Amendment) Act, No.       of 2024.

5

2. Section  48A  of  the  principal  enactment  is  hereby
amended  by  the  repeal  of  the  words  “or  upon  his  or  her
marriage” in subsection 48A 2(c).

3. Section  48B  of  the  principal  enactment  is  hereby

amended by the repeal of subsection 48B 1(a).

4. Section  68  of  the  principal  enactment  is  hereby
amended in subsection (1) of that section by the substitution
for the words “fails to succeed” of the words “fails without
reasonable cause to succeed”.

5.
