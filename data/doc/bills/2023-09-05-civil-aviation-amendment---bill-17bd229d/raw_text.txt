STATEMENT  OF  LEGAL  EFFECT

Clause  2  :  This  clause  amends  paragraph  (e)  of  subsection  (3)  of  section  6  of  the  Civil
Aviation Act,  No.  14  of  2010,  and  the  legal  effect  of  the  section  as  amended  is  to  provide
for  more  than  one  Service  Provider  to  be  appointed  for  supplying  aeronautical  services
referred  to  in  that  paragraph  and  to  remove  the  limitation  relating  to  the  appointment  of
Service  Provider,  such  being  the  appointment  of  public  corporation  or  a  company  in
which  the  Government  holds  a  majority  of  shares  as  the  Service  Provider.
