- Presented by Hon. Eran Wickramaratne, M.P. on July 18, 2023
- Published in the Gazette on July 07, 2023
- Contains amendments to the Special Commodity Levy Act, No. 48 of 2007
- Key amendment: Orders by the Minister per sections 2 or 5 of the Act will only come into force upon Parliament's approval
- Amends section 7 of the principal enactment, replacing paragraphs (i) to (iv) with new paragraphs (i) and (ii)
- New paragraphs mandate that Orders be published in the Gazette and placed before Parliament for approval before coming into force
- In case of any inconsistency between the Sinhala and Tamil texts, the Sinhala text prevails
- Bill No. 189, printed by the Department of Government Printing, Sri Lanka, and available for purchase and download.
