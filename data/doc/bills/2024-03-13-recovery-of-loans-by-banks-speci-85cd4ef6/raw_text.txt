STATEMENT OF LEGAL EFFECT

Clause 2:  This  clause  amends  the  Recovery  of  Loans  by  Banks  (Special  Provisions)  Act,
No. 4 of 1990 by inserting immediately after section 4 of that Act, new section 4A and the legal
effect as amended is to suspend, the selling of any property mortgaged to the bank as security for
any  loan  in  respect  of  which  default  has  been  made,  by  public  auction  during  the  period
commencing on the date of commencement of section 4A and ending on 15th December, 2024.
