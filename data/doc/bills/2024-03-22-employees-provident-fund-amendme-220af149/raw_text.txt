STATEMENT  OF  LEGAL  EFFECT

Clause  2  :  This  clause  amends  section  5  of  the  Employees’  Provident  Fund
Act,  No.  15  of  1958  (hereinafter  referred  to  as  the  “principal  enactment”),  and  the  legal
effect  of  the  amendment  is  to  ensure  adequate  and  timely  publication  and  disclosure  of  the
information  required  to  be  published  by  sub-paragraph  (h)  of  paragraph  (1)  thereof.

Clause  3  :  This  clause  inserts  the  Schedules  corresponding  to  the  amendments  to

sub-paragraph  (h)  of  paragraph  (1)  of  section  5.
