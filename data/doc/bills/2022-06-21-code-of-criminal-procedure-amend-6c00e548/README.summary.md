- Bill to amend the Code of Criminal Procedure Act, No. 15 of 1979.
- Presented by the Minister of Justice, Prison Affairs and Constitutional Reforms on June 21, 2022.
- Published in the Gazette on March 31, 2022.
- Bill No. 122, priced at Rs. 12.00, postage Rs. 15.00, available for download at www.documents.gov.lk.

**Key Amendments:**
- **Clause 2:** Amends Section 281 to ensure the court can pronounce sentences according to Section 53 of the Penal Code for persons under 18 at the time of committing an offense punishable by death.
- **Clause 3:** Amends Section 286 as a consequence of Clause 4â€™s amendments.
- **Clause 4:** Amends Section 288 to allow detainment of persons sentenced under Section 53 of the Penal Code in institutions established for those under 18.
