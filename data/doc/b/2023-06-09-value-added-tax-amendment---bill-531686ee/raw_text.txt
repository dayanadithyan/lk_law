STATEMENT  OF  LEGAL  EFFECT

This  Bill  amends  section  2A  of  the  Principal  Enactment  so  as  to  ensure  that  any  Order
made  by  the  Minister  thereunder  will  come  into  force  only  upon  the  approval  thereof  by
Parliament.
