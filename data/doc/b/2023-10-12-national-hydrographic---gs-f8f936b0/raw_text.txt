5

Preamble

AND WHEREAS the Government of Sri Lanka acceded to
the International Convention for the Safety of Life at Sea,
1974 on August 30, 1983:

AND WHEREAS although the Merchant Shipping Act, No.
52 of 1971 as amended by Act, No. 17 of 2019 has already
given effect to the International Convention for the Safety of
Life  at  Sea,  1974,  it  is  necessary  to  make  specific  legal
provisions to give effect to Sri Lanka’s obligations in relation
to  Regulations  4  and  9  of  Chapter  V  of  the  aforesaid
Convention:
