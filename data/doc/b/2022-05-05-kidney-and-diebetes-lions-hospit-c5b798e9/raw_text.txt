AN ACT TO INCORPORATE THE  KIDNEY AND DIABETES
LIONS HOSPITAL TRUST

WHEREAS a Foundation called and known as the “Kidney
and  Diabetes  Lions  Hospital  Trust”  has  heretofore  been
formed in Sri Lanka for the purpose of effectually  carrying
out  its objects and transacting all matters connected with the
said Trust according to the rules agreed to by its members:

5

Preamble

AND WHEREAS the said Trust has heretofore successfully
carried out and transacted the several objects and matters for
which it was established, and has  applied to be incorporated
and it will be expendient to grant the said application:
