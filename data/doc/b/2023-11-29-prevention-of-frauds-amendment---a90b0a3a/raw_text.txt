Statement of Legal Effect

Clause 2 : This clause amends section 2 of the Prevention
of Frauds Ordinance (Chapter 70) and the legal effect of the
section as amended is to provide a procedure which would
facilitate the prevention of forgery in respect of the execution
of a deed of transfer.
