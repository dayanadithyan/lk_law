STATEMENT  OF  LEGAL  EFFECT

Clause  2: This  clause  amends  section  2  of  the  Revocation  of  Irrevocable  Deeds  of  Gift
on  the  Ground  of  Gross  Ingratitude  Act,  No.  5  of  2017  (hereinafter  referred  to  as  the
“principal  enactment”)  and  the  legal  effect  of  the  section  as  amended  is  to  make  provision
to  revoke  an  irrevocable  deed  of  gift  on  the  ground  of  gross  ingratitude,  only  on  an  order
made  by  a  competent  court.

Clause  3: This  clause  replaces  section  3  of  the  principal  enactment  and  the  legal  effect
of  the  section  as  amended  is  to  reduce  the  time  period  to  three  years  within  which  an  action
shall  be  filed  to  revoke  an  irrevocable  deed  of  gift  on  the  ground  of  gross  ingratitude.

Clause  4: This  clause  replaces  section  4  of  the  principal  enactment  and  the  legal  effect
of  the  section  as  amended  is  to  specify  a  comprehensive  procedure  for  the  registration  of
lis  pendens.

Clause  5: This  clause  amends  section  6  of  the  principal  enactment  for  the  purpose  of

clarity.
