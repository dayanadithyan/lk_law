STATEMENT  OF  LEGAL  EFFECT

Clause    2  :  This  clause  inserts  new  section  154A  in  the  Civil  Procedure  Code
(Chapter  101)  and  the  legal  effect  of  the  section  is  to  exempt  certain  documents  from
formal  proof  at  any  proceedings  under  this  Code.
